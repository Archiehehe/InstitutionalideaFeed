# Product terminology

## Research Feed
- /feed
- source-verified institutional research articles
- ticker count is a signal, not always required
- no media summaries
- no conviction lists

## List Feed / Conviction Lists
- /conviction-lists
- named stock-pick lists with member tickers
- 3+ ticker rule remains unchanged
- media summaries are allowed as needs-review evidence
- not returned by /api/articles

## Sell-Side Finder
- /sell-side-list-finder
- intake workflow for creating conviction lists
